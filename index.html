The Enmore Inn

Great food and drink in the Heart of the Somerset Countryside

Home Cooked Food


At The Enmore Inn all of our food is freshly prepared and cooked by our resident chef, using locally sourced meat and produce.  We also have a variety of  special dishes available which are served daily, these are displayed on the specials boards inside.

Local Produce

We use only the freshest, local ingredients sourced from farms and trusted suppliers in the South West. Cooking using the freshest ingredients ensures all of our food tastes great and is of the highest quality.

Beers and Ales

The Enmore Inn hosts a wide variety of local Beers and Ales brewed in and around Somerset and the South West. 

Great Atmosphere

At The Enmore Inn Sarah and Mark make every effort to leave customers satified with their dining experience and aim to deliver the very best customer service possible.
